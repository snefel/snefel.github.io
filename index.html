Services

Work Process

  • Goal Discuss

    Our online marketing strategists specialize in understanding our client’s online business objectives and applying them online through intelligent planning, content development and media tactics - a combination of creativity and technology.

  • Strategy Planning

    We don’t work on pre-fixed packages. Based on the goals and the target audience of a business, we come up with the right digital marketing mix.

  • Execution

    Our automated tools creates an ads on behalf of you. We post them on various digital marketing channels and evaluate performance on daily basis. We not only evaluate performance but, we take corrective actions and optimize performance to get best results.

  • Monitoring

    We do not keep anything secrete. We download real time performance data from all the channels and publish it to our clients. Through our transparency reports, clients can view all the performance metrics through interactive dashboard.

Our Service

  • Search Engine Marketing

    Rather than trying to turn each and every visitor into a customer, it is better that you focus on the specific type of customer. We try to drive more targeted audience through search engine marketing. It helps to increase the visibility for clients through search results. We support search ads and display ads on search engines like Google, Bing.

  • Social Media Marketing

    We have built our company around our clients by taking the time to personalize their social media campaigns to fit their needs. We are a full-service firm offering Facebook, Twitter, YouTube and other social media outlets to ensure your business maximizes its reach via multiple channels.

  • Conversion Rate Optimization

    We download and monitor reports on daily basis. We capture metrics and try to analyze different variables like clicks, impression etc. Based on captured metrics, we shuffle budgets across channels to maximize ROI.

    Our business model works on the principle that gradually tries to decrease the click cost. By analysing the historical data, we try to find best possible bid amount for your campaigns through our machine learning algorithms.

    We help you to get backlinks for you website to improve the ranking of your site. Along with this we identify the broken outgoing links on your website. Snefel put your products or services in front of potential customers through popular bloggers or people with a large social media following.

    We help you to select the best keyword targets to raise your position in the search results of each of the major search engines. We will work with you in order to maximize your SEO potential.
